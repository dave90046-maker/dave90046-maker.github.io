Riskworld 2026 — Ebix Risk & RCS

Senior Marketing Officer / Brand & Creative Lead · Ebix, Inc. · 2025–2026

Role
Senior Marketing Officer / Brand & Creative Lead
Employer
Ebix, Inc. · 2025–2026
Collaborators
Brand manager, Claude AI
Tools
Adobe InDesign, Figma, Claude AI

Ebix's largest annual trade show, Riskworld 2026, served as the launch platform for four new risk management products across the Ebix Risk and RCS divisions. I built the full pre-show and on-site campaign from scratch—including a LinkedIn thought leadership carousel, direct mail campaign, five product brochures, an event landing page, VIP cocktail reception collateral, and a trade show booth redesign—while simultaneously running the EbixEnterprise product launch campaign for the Ebix Health division. Copy developed in collaboration with the brand manager using AI-assisted production tools.
