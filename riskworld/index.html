Riskworld 2026 — Ebix Risk & RCS

Senior Marketing Officer / Brand & Creative Lead · Ebix, Inc. · 2025–2026

Role
Senior Marketing Officer / Brand & Creative Lead
Employer
Ebix, Inc. · 2025–2026
Collaborators
Brand manager, Claude AI
Tools
Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Figma, Claude AI

Ebix's largest annual trade show, Riskworld 2026, served as the launch platform for a suite of risk management products across the Ebix Risk and RCS divisions. I built the full pre-show and on-site campaign from scratch—including a LinkedIn thought leadership carousel, direct mail campaign, five product brochures, an event landing page, VIP cocktail reception collateral, and a trade show booth redesign.
