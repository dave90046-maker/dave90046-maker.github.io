Zinnia Life & Annuity Relaunch

Senior Creative Designer & Marketing Coordinator · Zinnia · 2024

Role
Senior Creative Designer & Marketing Coordinator
Employer
Zinnia · 2024
Collaborators
Content writer, sales team
Tools
Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Figma, HubSpot

Following Zinnia's acquisition of Ebix's Life & Annuity division, the marketing team needed to rapidly reestablish the division's brand presence and reconnect with clients across print, digital, and event channels. Working from Zinnia's brand style guide and collaborating with content writers and product leadership, I adapted the brand system across a full suite of sales collateral, large-format trade show environments, and a LinkedIn webinar campaign series promoting weekly product demos to a ~200,000-contact list—lifting leads approximately 30%.
