EbixEnterprise Product Launch — Ebix Health

Senior Marketing Officer / Brand & Creative Lead · Ebix, Inc. · 2025–2026

Role
Senior Marketing Officer / Brand & Creative Lead
Employer
Ebix, Inc. · 2025–2026
Collaborators
Brand manager, content writer
Tools
Adobe InDesign, Figma, Claude AI

Ebix Health needed to relaunch EbixEnterprise—an enterprise benefits administration platform covering Quote to Card, Policy & Premium Administration, and Claims Administration—to a market of carriers, TPAs, payers, and self-insured groups. This campaign ran concurrently with Riskworld, using a shared visual language adapted across a content marketing and digital-first channel mix. Deliverables included a LinkedIn social card series, a product brochure suite, a platform lifecycle infographic, and four live thought leadership pages on ebixhealth.com.
